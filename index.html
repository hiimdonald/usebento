How it works

three taps · zero filing
STEP 01

Drop it in. No filing required.

Use the iOS share sheet, the in-app camera, voice recorder, or quick-note. Works from Safari, Instagram, Photos, Messages, anywhere. One tap and it's in your bento.

STEP 02

It knows what it is.

Each item is recognized and visually organized: a recipe shows ingredients and cook time, a dropped pin opens in Maps, a voice memo plays back with its waveform. Each thing looks like what it actually is.

STEP 03

Search the details, not the filename.

Bento reads the text inside every screenshot on your device, never ours. On devices with Apple Intelligence enabled, search gets even smarter. Search by a word, a phrase, or a detail you half-remember, without opening a single app.

05

Questions

asked & answered
Do I need an account to use Bento?

No. Install and start saving immediately: no email, no sign-in, no profile. If you want to sync across devices, Bento uses iCloud through your existing Apple ID. Nothing routes through our servers.

How can Bento search inside my screenshots?

Bento uses Apple's on-device text recognition (OCR) to read and index the words inside your images, all on your device, never uploaded. Once scanned, you can search any screenshot by the text it contains, just like a document.

What can I add to Bento?

Anything that shows up in the iOS share sheet: recipes, links, screenshots, photos, voice memos, PDFs, articles, places, contacts, Instagram posts, and more. Bento auto-detects the type — it knows what it is.

What's the difference between Lock and Hide?

Lock means a compartment is visible but gated by Face ID. Hide means the items don't appear in your inbox, search, or widgets at all. They live in a private vault you reveal with a pull-down gesture (or only from Settings, your choice).

Is Bento paid?

The core experience is free: up to 200 items, 20 boxes, 1 shared box, and iCloud sync, no account required. Bento Pro is available as an annual subscription ($4.99/year, free trial included) or a one-time Founding Member purchase ($19.99). Both unlock unlimited items, unlimited boxes, unlimited shared boxes, and every platform Bento ships on. The lifetime price will increase as features ship.

What devices does Bento support?

iPhone running iOS 26 or later. iPad and Mac are on the roadmap. Android is not currently planned.

Does Bento use Apple Intelligence?

Yes, where it's available. On iPhone 16 and later with Apple Intelligence enabled in Settings, Bento uses it to improve voice transcription and screenshot search. On older devices, Bento falls back to Apple's standard on-device tools, which still work well.
